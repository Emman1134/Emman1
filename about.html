About Emmanuel Adediran

Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Emmanuel is a 22 year old student currently studying Graphic Design at the British Columbia Institute of Technlogy. Since the age of 14, he has always had a passion for anyting media, whether it's Video Editing, Music Production, Livestreaming and so on. About 2 years ago was when he developed a passion for Graphic Design, started off by making clothes which then led to switching his program to get a degree for in Graphic Design. Some of his hobbies include playing Basketball, photography, making beats, and a lot of writing. Even with the hopeful getting his degree, Emmanuel tends to have some type of role in each of his hobbies and skills and make a name for himself in many different ways.
